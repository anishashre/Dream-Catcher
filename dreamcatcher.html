About Us

Dream catchers are traditionally handmade objects that originated from Native American cultures. They are believed to protect the sleeper from negative dreams, while letting positive dreams through. At Dream Catcher, we are passionate about preserving the cultural heritage of Native American tribes through the art of dream catchers. "Dream Catcher was born out of a deep admiration for Native American traditions and a desire to keep these ancient practices alive in the modern world. Dream catchers were originally created by the Ojibwe people to protect sleepers from negative dreams, allowing only positive dreams to pass through. Respect for indigenous cultures,authenticity in craftsmanship, and a commitment to ethical sourcing are at the heart of everything we do. Our team is a group of artisans and cultural enthusiasts dedicated to preserving the art of dream catchers. Our dream catchers have brought peace and positivity to countless homes.
